Our Story

Light is our
language, beauty
is our vision

Founded in 2012, POORVI Photography has become the trusted visual partner for discerning clients who demand artistry, precision, and authenticity. Our team of award-winning photographers brings a cinematic sensibility to every session — whether an intimate bridal portrait or a global brand campaign.

We believe every frame has the power to stop time. Our craft is built on deep technical mastery, genuine human connection, and an obsessive attention to light, composition, and emotion.

How We Work

Our creative process

01
Discovery Call

We begin with a deep conversation to understand your vision, goals, and the emotions you want to preserve forever.

02
Creative Brief

Our creative directors develop a tailored mood board, shot list, and concept that aligns with your unique aesthetic.

03
The Session

On shoot day we create a relaxed, inspired atmosphere so every frame feels natural, authentic, and effortlessly beautiful.

04
Delivery

Meticulously edited images delivered in a private online gallery within 2–3 weeks, ready to print and share.

The Founder

Satheesh Kumar

Founder & Lead Photographer

With a passion for storytelling through light and composition, Satheesh Kumar founded POORVI Photography with a singular vision — to craft images that transcend the ordinary and become timeless keepsakes.

His eye for authentic emotion and cinematic detail has earned the trust of hundreds of clients across weddings, corporate campaigns, and editorial projects. Every frame he captures reflects a deep commitment to artistry, precision, and genuine human connection.
