Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 March 2026 | Last Updated: 20 March 2026

POORVI Photography Studio ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website or engage our services in India.

1. Applicability and Legal Basis

This policy is intended to align with applicable Indian laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), the Information Technology Act, 2000, and related rules, to the extent applicable.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity details: name, phone number, and email address.
  • Event details: dates, location, type of event, preferences, and communication records.
  • Payment and billing details required to process your bookings.
  • Media and content: photographs, videos, and related creative material.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser details, and usage logs for site security and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to enquiries and provide photography services.
  • To schedule, deliver, and support booked sessions and projects.
  • To process payments, invoices, and legal compliance requirements.
  • To improve website performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To send service-related updates and, where lawful, promotional communications.

4. Consent and Withdrawal

Where required, we process personal data based on your consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us, subject to legal or contractual obligations that require retention or continued processing.

5. Data Sharing

We may share data only as necessary with:

  • Trusted vendors and service providers (hosting, payment, communication, and editing support).
  • Professional advisers and auditors where required.
  • Government or law enforcement authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed for service delivery, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements. Portfolio content is retained according to contractual terms and consent preferences.

7. Data Security

We maintain reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:

  • Access to your personal data.
  • Correction or updating of inaccurate information.
  • Erasure of personal data, where legally permissible.
  • Withdrawal of consent and grievance redressal.

9. Children

If we process data of minors, we do so with consent/authorization from a parent or lawful guardian as required under applicable law.

10. Cross-Border Processing

Where data is processed outside India through service providers, we ensure appropriate contractual and security controls, subject to legal requirements.
